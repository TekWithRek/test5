Drawing at a young age in front of cartoons, I realized early that I had a talent. Art has been a lifelong journey for me but at times I have strayed from it. Talked down to and discouraged, I ended up a laborer/builder. Happier work has been accomplished playing music. However, I have always been driven to create art; and learn and grow! For when it comes to labours of love, this is truly my first.
